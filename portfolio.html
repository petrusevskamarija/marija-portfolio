GASTRO. Prototype Case Study

GASTRO is a cutting-edge restaurant application tailored for culinary enthusiasts who seek specialized meals. The app allows users to order unique dishes, reserve tables for events, and enjoy a personalized dining experience.

The restaurant industry is increasingly leaning towards digital solutions to enhance customer experiences. However, many existing applications focus on general dining options without catering to niche markets. GASTRO was created to fill this gap, offering a platform where users can explore and order specialized meals and reserve tables for exclusive dining experiences.

Tiny Ones. Prototype Case Study

Tiny Ones is a specialized e-commerce platform dedicated to providing a wide range of high-quality products for babies and toddlers.

The baby product market is highly competitive, with parents seeking the best quality, safety, and convenience for their children. Tiny Ones was established to meet these demands by offering a comprehensive selection of baby products, including clothing, toys, furniture, and accessories, all under one roof.

Tiny Ones company is well known by the campaign ‘WE CARE’ which is a comprehensive breast cancer awareness campaign designed to educate, support, and inspire action in the fight against breast cancer. The campaign aims to raise awareness about the importance of early detection, provide resources for those affected, and promote a sense of community and solidarity.

Learn And Play. Prototype Case Study

Learn And Play is an educational app designed for children aged 3 to 6. The app combines interactive learning activities, games, and personalized feedback to create an engaging and effective educational experience.

In recent years, there has been a significant increase in the use of digital tools for education. However, many apps either focus on entertainment with little educational value or offer content that is not engaging for young learners. Learn And Play was developed to bridge this gap, providing a platform where children can learn essential skills in a fun and interactive way for free.

FORMA Kumanovo. Prototype Case Study

FORMA Kumanovo is an organization dedicated to promoting fine arts through the creation of graffiti and murals across Macedonia. The organization also hosts the annual Graffiti Fest Kumanovo and conducts workshops for children to nurture their artistic talents.

Graffiti and mural art have long been powerful forms of expression, transforming urban landscapes and engaging communities. FORMA Kumanovo was established to leverage these art forms to beautify public spaces, celebrate cultural identity, and inspire young artists. The organization's website serves as a hub for showcasing their work, promoting events, and engaging with the community.
